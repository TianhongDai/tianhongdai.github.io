Research

  • Overcoming Exploration Difficulities in the Sparse Reward Environments
  • Improving Generalisation Ability of DRL Agents
  • Interpretability of DRL Agents
  • Offline DRL
  • Language Conditioned Reinforcement Learning
  • 3D Structure Tracking in Medical/Microscopy Images by using DRL
